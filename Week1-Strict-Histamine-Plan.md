# Week 1 — STRICT (Very-Low Histamine) Day-by-Day

**Mon 20 – Sun 26 July 2026.** A 2–4 week strict elimination starts here; this is week 1. The goal is a clean read on whether very-low histamine does anything for your **focus/brain-fog and bloating** (log both daily — this week *is* the experiment). After week 2–4 you drop to LOW tier.

Recipes referenced (e.g. "recipe 4") are in `Low-Histamine-Recipes.md`. Strict rules recap: **ultra-fresh protein, cook fresh, eat within ~1 hr, no reheating leftovers, no citrus/tomato/spinach/avocado, no fermented/aged/cured/smoked, peel veg, salt + fresh herbs only.**

---

## Read these three notes first

1. **Protein scenario (kidney).** Portions below are the **normal scenario (~180–200 g protein/day)**. **If your cystatin C eGFR confirms CKD, cut every animal-protein portion by ~40% (target 110–130 g/day)** and add more rice/pumpkin to hold calories. Don't guess — this is roadmap open-question #1.
2. **Strict = fresh cooking.** The honest cost of strict tier is more cooking. Where a meal can't be cooked fresh, the compromise is **cook → freeze in single portions within 30 min → thaw overnight → reheat once**. Purest strict skips even that one reheat; do what's realistic for a 5:30am schedule and note in your log which meals were fresh vs reheated.
3. **Buy meat fresh, freeze immediately.** Shop **Sun 19** and again **Thu 23** for the freshest possible protein. Freeze what you won't cook in 48 hr *the day you buy it*. Fish only if you can get it same-day ultra-fresh (or frozen-at-sea) — otherwise sub chicken; fish is the most histamine-prone food there is.

## Daily tracking (this is the point of the week)

Log each night (roadmap §8): **focus/brain-fog 1–10**, **bloating 1–10**, sleep 1–10, bowel transit (formed?), and whether each meal was **fresh-cooked or reheated**. At the end of week 1, look at the focus and bloating lines against "strict mode ON." That trend — not any label — tells you whether this is worth continuing.

## Strict-week watch-outs
- **No citrus/tomato/spinach/avocado/banana** slipped into anything — the usual sabotage.
- **No leftovers eaten cold from the fridge 2–3 days later** — that's the histamine trap this whole week is built to avoid.
- **Fish only if ultra-fresh.** When in doubt, chicken.
- **Bone broth stays swapped** for short-cook stock all week.
- **Watch the plan's other flags:** no onion/garlic this week (methanogen); bone-broth tolerance (using stock instead); if you get headaches/jitter after the AM stock, drop to 100 ml or use plain L-glutamine in water.

*Not medical advice — organises an experiment you've chosen to run. Keep your prescriber in the loop on the peptide/injection schedule and the eGFR finding.*
